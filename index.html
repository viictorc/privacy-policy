Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Career Copilot ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome Extension that helps users tailor resume bullet points to job descriptions using AI.

Data We Collect

Career Copilot collects minimal data:

  • Resume content and job descriptions — stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. Never stored on our servers.
  • License key — stored locally on your device. Sent to our server only to verify your subscription status.
  • Anonymous usage ID — a randomly generated ID stored locally, used only to track your free tier usage count (3 free analyses per month).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information through the extension.
  • We do not track your browsing history or web activity.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI — resume bullets and job description text are sent to OpenAI's API to generate suggestions. OpenAI's privacy policy applies.
  • LemonSqueezy — handles payment processing. We receive your email address from LemonSqueezy when you subscribe, used only to deliver your license key.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: caoran213@gmail.com
